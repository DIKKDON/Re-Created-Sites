ENVIRONMENTAL, SOCIAL, AND GOVERNANCE

What is ESG?

ESG stands for environmental, social, and governance. Acorns
Sustainable Portfolios are made of Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs)
designed to provide exposure to more sustainable companies, while
aiming to perform on par with a traditional portfolio.

Environmental

How a company meets standards
related to the environment, like
renewable energy, animal welfare,
and reducing CO2 emissions.

Social

How a company meets standards
related to social topics, like
corporate social responsibility,
supporting local communities,
and data security.

Governance

How a company meets standards
related to governance, like anti-
corruption, diverse board of
directors, and ethics.

Why Acorns Sustainable Portfolios?

Acorns Sustainable Portfolios are built with iShares, the world’s leading
innovator of sustainable ETFs. They’re designed to perform on par with
our Core portfolios, and help our customers build a strong foundation for
the long term.

ESG Fun Fact

At the height of the COVID crisis, net new
assets into ESG ETFs grew by $900 million!
Over half went into iShares broad ESG ETFs,
like those in Acorns Sustainable Portfolios.
This signals that investors are seeking
companies with ESG-friendly methods and
innovations.

Learn more about
ESG Ratings

Global ESG ratings are set by MSCI, a
leading provider of research and data, that
assesses a company’s response to some of
the world’s biggest issues. Companies with
positive ESG business practices may be
better positioned for long term growth.

ESG Performance

A study of 3,000 U.S. mutual funds and ETFs
found that sustainable equity funds
outperformed non-ESG peer funds by a
median total return of 4.3% in 2020. There
are no guarantees in investing, but experts
cite consumer demand for sustainability as a
positive, long-term indicator for ESG investing.
